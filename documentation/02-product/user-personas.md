# User Personas

## Purpose

This document defines the primary users the platform must serve.

Personas are not stereotypes. They are product design tools used to clarify goals, constraints, workflows, and success measures.

## Persona 1: Young Beginner Learner

Age band:

- 8-12.

Context:

- learns through school, club, parent, or coding academy,
- may use shared devices,
- may need visual and game-based learning,
- may have limited typing speed.

Goals:

- build games and animations,
- understand basic logic,
- feel successful early,
- share projects safely.

Needs:

- block coding,
- visual feedback,
- simple language,
- safe AI hints,
- teacher/guardian oversight.

Risks:

- frustration from complex UI,
- unsafe public sharing,
- overdependence on AI answers.

Success metrics:

- completes first project,
- runs project multiple times,
- requests hints without abandoning,
- can explain sequence/loop/event basics.

## Persona 2: Teen Coding Learner

Age band:

- 13-18.

Context:

- wants practical digital skills,
- may be preparing for school exams, competitions, or future career,
- may use mobile-first internet.

Goals:

- learn real code,
- build portfolio projects,
- transition from blocks to JavaScript/Python,
- receive guidance when stuck.

Needs:

- block-to-code progression,
- browser IDE,
- AI coding mentor,
- projects,
- quizzes,
- career roadmap.

Success metrics:

- completes Level 2/3 projects,
- submits code,
- improves from AI/teacher feedback,
- demonstrates competencies.

## Persona 3: Adult Career Switcher

Context:

- wants employable skills,
- may work/study part-time,
- likely values clear career outcomes.

Goals:

- become job-ready,
- learn Laravel/React/Next.js/Flutter/cloud/AI,
- build credible portfolio,
- prepare for interviews.

Needs:

- structured roadmap,
- browser IDE,
- AI career coach,
- code review,
- projects,
- certificates,
- flexible pacing.

Success metrics:

- completes portfolio projects,
- improves code quality,
- passes competency assessments,
- reaches career readiness milestones.

## Persona 4: Classroom Teacher

Context:

- teaches many learners,
- may not be a senior software engineer,
- needs classroom visibility and time-saving tools.

Goals:

- assign lessons,
- monitor progress,
- support struggling learners,
- reduce preparation time,
- produce reports.

Needs:

- class dashboard,
- assignment creation,
- AI lesson/quiz drafts,
- rubrics,
- intervention alerts,
- learner progress summaries.

Risks:

- distrusts AI output,
- too much dashboard complexity,
- slow onboarding.

Success metrics:

- creates first class,
- assigns first lesson,
- reviews learner progress weekly,
- reports time saved.

## Persona 5: Institution Admin

Context:

- manages school, bootcamp, university department, NGO, or training center.

Goals:

- onboard teachers and learners,
- manage subscriptions,
- track outcomes,
- prove value,
- align curriculum.

Needs:

- tenant administration,
- bulk import,
- reports,
- subscription management,
- curriculum settings,
- data exports,
- AI usage control.

Success metrics:

- institution activates learners,
- teachers use dashboard,
- completion improves,
- renewal intent is positive.

## Persona 6: Parent Or Guardian

Context:

- supports a child learner,
- may pay for subscription,
- wants safety and progress visibility.

Goals:

- know child is learning safely,
- view achievements,
- understand progress,
- control payments.

Needs:

- child progress summary,
- billing,
- certificates,
- safe notifications,
- teacher-approved reports.

Success metrics:

- parent links child,
- views report,
- understands value,
- renews subscription.

## Persona 7: Curriculum Author

Context:

- creates or improves learning content.

Goals:

- produce high-quality curriculum,
- map skills and competencies,
- generate drafts faster with AI,
- publish reviewed content.

Needs:

- authoring tools,
- AI draft generation,
- review workflow,
- versioning,
- standards mapping,
- accessibility checklist.

Success metrics:

- content passes review,
- learner outcomes improve,
- fewer content defects.

## Persona 8: Platform Operations Admin

Context:

- monitors platform health, support, security, AI usage, and billing.

Goals:

- keep platform reliable,
- resolve incidents,
- control AI cost,
- manage institutions,
- audit sensitive actions.

Needs:

- admin console,
- AI Mission Control,
- logs/metrics,
- billing tools,
- support tools,
- audit trails.

Success metrics:

- incidents resolved quickly,
- AI cost stays within budget,
- support SLAs met,
- security issues tracked.

## Cross-Persona Requirements

- role-based access,
- clear onboarding,
- accessible UI,
- transparent AI,
- low-bandwidth tolerance,
- mobile-responsive design,
- support and recovery paths,
- privacy-by-design.
