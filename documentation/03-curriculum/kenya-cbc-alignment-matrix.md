# Kenya CBC Alignment Matrix

## Purpose

This document defines how the platform should align computing education content with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum/CBE context.

Official curriculum alignment must use Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development materials as the source reference.

Source:

- KICD CBC materials: https://kicd.ac.ke/cbc-materials/

## Important Boundary

The platform may claim "mapped to KICD/CBC learning areas" only after curriculum specialist review.

It should not claim official endorsement or approval unless formally granted by the relevant authority.

## Alignment Goals

- help Kenyan schools understand fit,
- support teacher planning,
- map computing concepts to relevant learning outcomes,
- support digital literacy, computational thinking, coding, robotics, and STEM activities,
- produce reports for institution partners.

## Alignment Fields

Each platform lesson/project should map:

```text
platform_level
platform_unit
lesson_or_project_id
platform_skill_ids
competency_ids
KICD_grade_band
learning_area
strand
sub_strand
specific_learning_outcome
suggested_learning_experience
assessment_rubric_reference
alignment_strength
reviewer
review_date
notes
```

Alignment strength:

- direct,
- partial,
- enrichment,
- career extension,
- not aligned.

## Initial Mapping Areas

Likely relevant areas:

- digital literacy,
- pre-technical/pre-career education,
- integrated science/STEM,
- mathematics reasoning,
- creative arts/media where animations/storytelling apply,
- computer studies/ICT where available by level,
- robotics/engineering enrichment.

Exact mapping requires review of current KICD curriculum designs for target grades.

## Level 1 Example Mapping

Platform topic:

- sequence and events in block coding.

Potential CBC relevance:

- computational thinking,
- problem solving,
- creativity,
- digital literacy,
- communication,
- collaboration.

Evidence needed:

- exact grade,
- exact learning area,
- exact strand/sub-strand,
- curriculum specialist confirmation.

## Teacher Support Outputs

For Kenyan teachers, the platform should generate:

- lesson objective mapping,
- suggested learning activities,
- assessment rubric,
- required resources,
- learner evidence,
- CBC-friendly report language.

## Review Workflow

```text
Select platform content
  |
  v
Find relevant KICD curriculum design
  |
  v
Map strand/sub-strand/outcome
  |
  v
Curriculum specialist review
  |
  v
Teacher pilot feedback
  |
  v
Publish alignment mapping
```

## Risks

- outdated curriculum documents,
- incorrect grade mapping,
- overclaiming alignment,
- confusing enrichment with required curriculum,
- teacher misinterpretation.

## Controls

- store source document version/date,
- require reviewer name,
- separate official curriculum outcomes from platform enrichment,
- review mappings annually or when KICD updates materials.

## Acceptance Criteria

- every Kenya alignment claim cites a KICD source,
- each mapping has reviewer and date,
- direct vs enrichment alignment is clear,
- schools can export alignment reports,
- curriculum changes trigger review.
