# Child Safety Policy

## Purpose

This document defines child safety principles for learners under the age of majority.

This is a product/security policy draft and must be legally reviewed before launch.

## Principles

- minimize child data,
- protect privacy,
- make AI age-appropriate,
- restrict unsafe sharing,
- give teachers/guardians appropriate oversight,
- avoid manipulative engagement patterns.

## Child Account Rules

- collect only necessary data,
- support school-authorized accounts,
- support guardian-linked accounts where required,
- restrict public profile visibility,
- restrict external publishing by default.

## AI Safety For Children

AI must:

- use age-appropriate language,
- scaffold instead of solving,
- avoid harmful or adult content,
- avoid pretending to be human,
- encourage asking teacher/guardian for serious issues,
- respect institution settings.

## Communication Rules

- no open direct messaging between unrelated adults and children,
- teacher/learner communication must be institution-scoped,
- parent/guardian reporting should be controlled and appropriate.

## Moderation

Moderate:

- uploaded images,
- project sharing,
- comments/community later,
- marketplace content later.

## Incident Response

Child safety incidents require:

- immediate triage,
- access restriction if needed,
- preservation of evidence,
- escalation to responsible owner,
- legal review where required.

## Acceptance Criteria

- child safety policy is reviewed before pilot,
- AI child safety rules are tested,
- public sharing is restricted,
- sensitive incidents have escalation path.
