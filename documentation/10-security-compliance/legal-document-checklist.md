# Legal Document Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist tracks legal and policy documents needed before launch, school pilots, paid subscriptions, child learners, AI processing, and institution partnerships.

This is not legal advice. A qualified lawyer should review all legal documents.

## Kenya-First References

- ODPC: https://www.odpc.go.ke/
- Kenya Data Protection Act reference: https://natlex.ilo.org/dyn/natlex2/r/natlex/fe/details?p3_isn=109333

## Required Before Public Launch

- Terms of Service,
- Privacy Notice,
- Acceptable Use Policy,
- Cookie Policy if cookies/tracking are used,
- AI Use Policy,
- Child Safety Policy,
- Support Policy,
- Refund/Cancellation Policy.

## Required Before Institution Pilots

- Pilot Agreement,
- Data Processing Agreement,
- Institution Admin Terms,
- Learner Data Handling Schedule,
- Support SLA for pilot,
- Consent/authorization workflow,
- Data export/deletion process.

## Required Before Paid Subscriptions

- Subscription Terms,
- Payment Terms,
- Invoice/receipt policy,
- Refund policy,
- Plan entitlement terms,
- M-Pesa/card payment processor terms.

## Required Before AI Features

- AI Data Handling Policy,
- Provider/Subprocessor Registry,
- AI Safety Policy,
- AI Output Disclaimer,
- Human Review Policy for high-impact outputs,
- AI Incident Response Process.

## Required Before Children Use Platform

- child learner privacy language,
- parent/guardian consent workflow where required,
- school-authorized account creation policy,
- age-appropriate AI policy,
- public sharing restrictions,
- moderation policy.

## Required Before Marketplace/Plugins

- creator terms,
- marketplace content policy,
- revenue share terms,
- plugin developer terms,
- security review policy,
- takedown policy,
- IP/copyright policy.

## Data Protection Review Questions

- Are we a data controller, processor, or both?
- Do we need ODPC registration?
- What categories of personal data are processed?
- What child data is processed?
- What subprocessors are used?
- Where is data stored?
- How do users request access/deletion/export?
- How long are AI logs retained?

## Acceptance Criteria

- legal owner assigned,
- lawyer review completed,
- policies linked in product,
- consent flows implemented where required,
- institution agreements approved before pilot,
- subprocessor registry maintained.
