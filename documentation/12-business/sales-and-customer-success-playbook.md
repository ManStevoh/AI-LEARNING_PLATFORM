# Sales And Customer Success Playbook

## Purpose

This document defines how the platform should sell to, onboard, support, and renew institutions.

## Ideal Early Customers

- private schools with ICT/STEM interest,
- coding academies,
- NGOs running digital skills programs,
- TVET/university innovation departments,
- after-school STEM clubs.

## Discovery Questions

- How do you currently teach coding/digital skills?
- How many learners and teachers would use this?
- What devices/internet are available?
- What curriculum alignment matters?
- What reports do administrators need?
- What budget range is realistic?

## Demo Structure

- learner journey,
- block coding project,
- AI mentor,
- teacher dashboard,
- institution reporting,
- subscription/implementation plan.

## Customer Success

Ongoing support:

- teacher training,
- term planning,
- usage reports,
- support escalation,
- renewal review.

Health indicators:

- active teachers,
- active learners,
- assignments created,
- project completion,
- support issues,
- leadership engagement.

## Renewal Package

Include:

- usage summary,
- learning outcomes,
- teacher feedback,
- project examples,
- recommended next plan,
- pricing proposal.

## Acceptance Criteria

- sales process is repeatable,
- pilot outcomes are measurable,
- customer success owner is assigned,
- renewal conversation uses evidence.
