# Field Research And Interview Plan

## Purpose

This document defines how to validate the platform with schools, teachers, learners, parents, and institutions.

## Research Goals

- validate school pain points,
- validate willingness to pay,
- understand device/connectivity constraints,
- understand teacher workflow,
- test AI trust,
- test curriculum fit,
- measure pilot readiness.

## Participant Groups

- school owners/admins,
- ICT/computer teachers,
- coding academy operators,
- learners,
- parents/guardians,
- NGOs,
- employers later.

## Interview Topics

School admins:

- budget,
- procurement,
- curriculum fit,
- reporting needs,
- teacher capacity,
- device/internet realities.

Teachers:

- preparation workload,
- assessment pain points,
- class sizes,
- AI concerns,
- dashboard needs.

Learners:

- motivation,
- frustration points,
- preferred devices,
- AI helpfulness,
- project interests.

Parents:

- safety,
- willingness to pay,
- progress visibility,
- career expectations.

## Evidence To Collect

- interview notes,
- survey results,
- prototype usability observations,
- pricing reactions,
- pilot commitments,
- objections,
- connectivity/device data.

## Research Ethics

- get consent,
- protect child participants,
- avoid collecting unnecessary personal data,
- anonymize findings where possible,
- separate opinion from measured behavior.

## Acceptance Criteria

- at least 10 teacher/admin interviews before pilot,
- at least 20 learner/parent feedback sessions before broad rollout,
- findings update product and pricing decisions,
- assumptions register is updated.
